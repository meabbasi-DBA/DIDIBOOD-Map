# Roadmap Management Skill

## Objectives
- Maintain a clear, prioritized product roadmap
- Align delivery timelines with business goals
- Communicate trade-offs between scope and schedule
- Balance short-term wins with long-term platform growth

## Responsibilities
- Organize features into phases and milestones
- Prioritize backlog items by business impact
- Track roadmap changes and rationale
- Coordinate release planning across teams
- Reassess priorities when constraints change

## Deliverables
- Quarterly and annual roadmaps
- Milestone definitions
- Release plans
- Priority change logs
- Stakeholder-facing roadmap summaries

## Roadmap Standards
- Group work into themes (e.g., core platform, analytics, UX)
- Label items as MVP, growth, or enterprise
- Include dependencies and blockers
- Review and update roadmap regularly
- Keep roadmap realistic and technically feasible
