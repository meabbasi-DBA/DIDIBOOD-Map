# Stakeholder Management Skill

## Objectives
- Align diverse stakeholders on product direction
- Manage expectations across business and engineering
- Facilitate decisions on scope, priority, and trade-offs
- Keep communication clear and decision-ready

## Responsibilities
- Identify key stakeholders and their interests
- Run requirement and roadmap review sessions
- Document decisions, dissent, and follow-ups
- Communicate status, risks, and changes proactively
- Resolve priority conflicts with data and business context

## Deliverables
- Stakeholder maps
- Decision records
- Status and change communications
- Meeting summaries with action items
- Escalation notes for unresolved blockers

## Management Standards
- Confirm decisions in writing
- Separate facts from opinions in discussions
- Escalate only when trade-offs need executive input
- Keep engineering unblocked with timely clarifications
- Balance business urgency with technical sustainability
